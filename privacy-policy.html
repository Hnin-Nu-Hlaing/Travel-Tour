Privacy Policy

At Just Travel Agency, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with us, whether through our website, services, or promotions.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you:

  • Book travel services
  • Sign up for our newsletter or promotional offers
  • Create an account or membership profile
  • Contact our customer service team
  • Interact with our website and social media platforms

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and manage your travel bookings and inquiries
  • Provide you with travel-related updates and information
  • Send you special offers, promotions, and discounts, especially tailored to our members
  • Improve our services and website functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

Member Promotions

At Just Travel Agency, we value our members and offer exclusive promotions to enhance your travel experience. When you sign up as a member, you’ll gain access to special offers and discounts that are not available to non-members.

As part of our loyalty promotion, once you’ve made three purchases through our agency, you will automatically receive a discount on your next booking. This is our way of saying thank you for choosing Just Travel Agency for your travel needs.

To stay informed about your progress and available discounts, make sure to sign up for our promotional emails, where we’ll keep you updated on exclusive offers and any new rewards you’re eligible for.You can view and manage your membership status anytime by logging into your account.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers to fulfill your travel bookings
  • Third-party vendors who assist us with business operations, such as payment processing and marketing services
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to ensure your personal information is protected. These include secure servers, encryption, and restricted access to personal data within our organization.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to make changes to your account or have questions about how your data is used, please contact our customer service team.

You may also choose to:

  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications
  • Request information about how we use your data
  • Withdraw consent for specific data processing activities

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather insights on website usage. Cookies help us personalize your experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain features. You can adjust your browser settings to manage cookie preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes via email or on our website.

Transportation Services

Meal Arrangements

Please note that Just Travel Agency does not provide meal services as part of our travel packages. However, during your journey, our travel buses will make scheduled stops near restaurants or food courts, allowing you to purchase and enjoy meals at your convenience.

While meals are not included in our service, we ensure that there are ample opportunities for you to dine comfortably at various stops along the route. You can explore local cuisine and choose meal options that suit your preferences.
